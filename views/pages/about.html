About ArtistGrade

Crafting Creativity, Empowering Artists Worldwide

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ArtistGrade emerged from a vision to transform how artists access high-quality materials and custom artworks. What started as a small local studio has grown into a global creative platform, serving thousands of creators and businesses.

Our team blends traditional craftsmanship with modern technology, ensuring every product inspires creativity and empowers artistic expression. From hand-crafted tools to personalized artworks, we deliver excellence at every step.

Our Mission

To provide premium, innovative art tools and personalized solutions that unlock every creator’s potential, making world-class artistry accessible to all.

Our Vision

To be the world’s leading platform for artists and creative professionals, inspiring a global community with tools, knowledge, and collaboration opportunities.

Our Core Values

Innovation

Constantly creating modern, efficient, and sustainable art solutions.

Quality

Delivering unmatched craftsmanship and premium materials in every product.

Community

Supporting a global network of artists through resources, workshops, and collaborations.

Our Journey

2020 – The Beginning

ArtistGrade was founded in a small studio with a vision to empower artists globally.

2021 – First Online Store

Launched our eCommerce platform to reach creators worldwide with premium art tools.

2023 – Global Expansion

Expanded operations to international markets, serving thousands of creators across continents.

2025 – Community First

Introduced workshops, collaborations, and artist support programs to strengthen the creative community.
